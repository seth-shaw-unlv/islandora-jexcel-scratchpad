The Spreadsheet

Notes

Currently assumes you have an Islandora instance accessible at localhost:8000 that has JSON:API and CORS (see below) enabled with some existing Controlled Access Terms populated.

Assumes both the csv_serialization and image_base64_formatter modules are installed on your Drupal site with a REST-based view exposed as a CSV file.

Only partially works right now. I can get the thumbnail and field types to work but column titles only work by setting csvHeaders to false and then deleting the first line. Column titles now work. The CSV csv_serialization module uses a quoted string with comma delimiters for multi-valued columns rather than the semi-colons used by jExcel. I haven't found a simple way to convince either of them to change their behavior on this point without PRs.

Initially my browser refused to load the dropdown boxes from Drupal due to Cross-Origin Resource Sharing (CORS). A bit of searching lead me to a forum post indicating that I needed to configure my site's services.yml to enable CORS. There may be a way to do it without this, but it is what I have right now.
